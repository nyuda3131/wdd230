Target Audience

Our target audience for the Kagoshima Chamber of Commerce website is primarily local businesses and residents, but we also aim to attract tourists and new businesses to the area. The website will provide information about the chamber, its members, and the benefits of joining as well as highlight local events, attractions, and businesses.

Personas

Persona 1: Local Business Owner

  • Name: Koyo Sonae
  • Age: 39
  • Occupation: Owner of a local restaurant
  • Goals: To increase business and connect with other local business owners
  • Challenges: Finding ways to stand out in a competitive market

Persona 2: Tourist

  • Name: Sarah
  • Age: 28
  • Occupation: Photographer
  • Goals: To find interesting things to do and places to eat while visiting Kagoshima
  • Challenges: Navigating a new city and finding authentic experiences

Scenarios

  1. Sarah wants to find out about upcoming local business events.
  2. Sarah is looking for recommendations for a unique dining experience.
  3. Koyo Sonae Yamamoto is interested in joining the chamber of commerce and wants to learn more about the benefits.
  4. Sarah is planning a trip to Kagoshima and wants to learn about local attractions.
  5. Koyo Sonae wants to network with other local business owners.
