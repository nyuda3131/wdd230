Color Scheme

The color scheme for the Kagoshima Chamber of Commerce website is influenced by the vibrant colors of the city's nature such as the greenery and blue waters.

The primary color used is green. The secondary color is blue. These colors are used consistently throughout the website to provide a consistant look and feel.

Typography

The typography for the Kagoshima Chamber of Commerce website is clean and modern with an emphasis on readability.

The heading font used is Montserrat, which is a clean and modern sans-serif font. The body font used is Open Sans, which is a legible and versatile sans-serif font. These fonts are used consistently throughout the website to provide a cohesive look and feel.
