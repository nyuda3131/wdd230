Membership Benefits:

NP Membership: No fee, suitable for non-profit organizations.

Silver Membership ($200): The Silver Level provides you with strategic networking and access. Engage with key decision makers and peers, explore best practices that advance the community. This membership level includes all Core Benefits and a broad range of enhanced engagement and exposure opportunities.

Gold Membership ($300): The Gold Level increases your influence among elected officials and access to your customers and peers. Help develop policies and positions on key issues affecting Utah`s community at the federal, state and local level.
